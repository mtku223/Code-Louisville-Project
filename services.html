FULL SERVICE PROCESS

We offer anything screen printing -to- embroidery. We have been a part of the service industry for over a decade, and we pride ourselves on listening to our customers and delivering exceptional results.

We walk you through getting your garments picked out. Then we make sure your graphics are situated. After that, we are off the races and you just have to sit back.

STANDARD PRINTING - PLASTISOL INKS

Our standard screen printing is done using traditional plastisol inks. We are capable of printing up to 10 colors on light garments, and up to 9 colors on dark garments that require an underbase print. Our standard prints are printed exclusively using pthalate free plastisol inks. These inks are manufactured to be the safest plastisol inks on the market. Our superior standard prints have a "soft -hand" feel and are often mistaken for water-based ink prints on light garments.

DISCHARGE

Discharge printing has become a very popular style of printing in today's apparel market. Unlike plastisol inks, discharge inks penetrate the fabric and evaporate the dye from the shirt, leaving your design in its place. Printing with discharge inks offers an extremely soft hand on dark shirts, ultimately having no feel at all once washed. For this one color print technique, we offer both standard discharge (off-white/cream in color) as well as white discharge ink. This type of printing requires 100% cotton garments,

4 COLOR PROCESS PRINTING

Four color process prints are the perfect way to achieve a full color image on white or natural shirts. By using translucent Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, and Black inks, we are able to reproduce full color images by blending different percentages of halftone dots together to get a full range of colors. Printing on darker garments is possible with an underbase of white, but best results come on a white shirt.

WATERBASED PRINTING

Printing with waterbased inks offers an extremely soft feel on light colored shirts. Rather than sitting on top of the shirt fabric like traditional plastisol inks, waterbased inks penetrate the fibers of the shirt, making the print one with the garment and should be exclusively printed on white shirts for best results. Since further steps are required for this type of printing, it does come at a premium charge. Reach out to one of our reps to explore waterbased inks on your next order.
